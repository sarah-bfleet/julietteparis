In 2017, the creators of the brand Juliette Paris, Anastasiia and Frederic Sourisse, drawing on more than 15 years of experience with diamonds, created the “Unique Piece” collection whose signature rests in colored natural diamonds. The success of the collection and the wishes of the customers prompted the creators of the brand to change their concept and produce high-end jewelry exclusively made-to-measure. Extensive experience and excellent knowledge of the precious stones market allow Anastasiia and Frederic to create Haute Joaillerie that can surprise even the most demanding client.
